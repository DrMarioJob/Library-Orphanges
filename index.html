Mission of the Orphanage Library

To establish a library for orphanages in Bali to provide children with access to knowledge and books, promote English language learning and develop the skills needed for successful employment in the future.

Our goal is to give every child a chance for a better future through education, reading culture and knowledge of foreign languages.

Education and knowledge for everyone:

Every child deserves the opportunity to receive a quality education, regardless of their social status. We strive to provide children with access to books that will help them learn, dream, and build their future.

Orphanage Library Values

English language learning:

Every child deserves the opportunity to receive a quality education, regardless of their social status. We strive to provide children with access to books that will help them learn, dream, and build their future.

Employment preparation:

Our library will help children develop skills that will be in demand in the job market. With English language skills and a broad outlook, they will be able to find jobs in areas such as hospitality, tourism, and other industries.

How we will
achieve
our goals:
1
Professional English lessons:

We hire local English teachers who conduct classes in orphanages, actively using books from our library for teaching. This not only improves reading and writing skills, but also helps children better understand foreign literature.

2
Free access to books:

Children will have constant access to the library, where they can read books of their choice. We also offer the opportunity to order books that interest children, so that the library is more relevant and motivates them to read.

3
Motivational classes on the culture of reading:

An important part of our program is educational classes that will help children realize the importance of reading books and proficiency in English. We will show children how books can develop their personality and help them achieve success in the future.

6
Teaching self-presentation skills:

We will teach children to introduce themselves, talk about themselves, their dreams and hobbies in English. This will help them feel more confident in the future, including when communicating with potential employers.

7
Expanding the program:

We will gradually increase the number of orphanages that can use the library, reaching more children and spreading knowledge more widely.

4
Surveying children:

We will conduct regular surveys to find out what books children are interested in. This will make the library more relevant and help maintain children's interest in reading.

5
Developing booklets:

We will create booklets that explain the importance of education and reading to children in simple and understandable language. The booklets will help children see the long-term benefits of reading and learning.

Value for children
from orphanages
Access to knowledge and development:

With the help of the library, children will be able to learn, broaden their horizons and prepare for independent life.

They will not only have access to books, but also the opportunity to discuss them, share opinions and develop critical thinking.

English as a tool for the future:

Knowledge of English will significantly increase children's chances of employmen in the future.

This is especially important in Bali, where the tourism industry is developing and there is a high demand for English-speaking specialists.

Development of self-organization and responsibility skills:

Children will learn to plan their time, choose books according to their interests and study independently, which contributes to their overall development.

Self-confidence:

Children who can freely introduce themselves in English and talk about their dreams will feel more confident, which will be an important element of their future employment.
